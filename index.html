Tugwell Master Builders is a Cardiff based building company serving South Wales and beyond. We aim to deliver customer satisfaction for every job we do, from high-end refurbishments to fixing a leaking tap.

We offer three distinct services:

Design & Build

We offer a bespoke Design & Build service. Utilising our trusted architect, as well as our fully regulated & certified team, we can offer our clients a tailor-made solution from planning to the final build. We take pride in remaining versatile during the entire process, allowing our clients to make adjustments even after construction has begun.

General Building

Refurbishments, Extensions, Structural Works, Basements, Timber Decking, Landscaping, Wood Burner / Log Burning Stove Installation – these are just a few of the General Building services we provide.

Minor Work

We provide a wide array of services to landlords and home owners. No job is too big, nor too small! If you’re in our local area, please contact us to carry out those ‘handyman’ tasks.
